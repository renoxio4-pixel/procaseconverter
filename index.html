Professional Text Case Converter Online

Easily convert your text between different cases: Sentence case, lower case, UPPER CASE, Capitalized Case, and more. Our tool is designed for students, writers, and developers who need to quickly format their content without manual editing.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do the conversion buttons work?
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes the first letter of sentences.
  • lower case: Converts all text to small letters.
  • UPPER CASE: Converts all text to CAPITAL letters.
  • Capitalized Case: Capitalizes the first letter of every word.
  • iNVERSE cASE: Swaps small to caps and vice versa.
Is this case converter safe and private?

Yes, Pro Case Converter is 100% safe. All conversions happen locally in your browser. We never store or upload your text to any server.

Tips for professional text formatting

Use 'Sentence case' for emails and blog posts to maintain readability. Use 'Capitalized Case' for headlines or titles. Use the 'Download' button to quickly save your formatted text without copying.
